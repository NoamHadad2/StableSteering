# Terminology Ledger

Use these canonical phrases consistently across the paper package.

## Paper identity

- preferred primary label: `traceable research platform`
- acceptable secondary shorthand: `replayable steering loop`
- avoid rotating between many near-synonyms unless the venue requires them

## Workflow descriptor

- preferred phrase: `prompt-first, preference-guided multi-round diffusion refinement`
- reserve `prompt-centric` and `prompt rewriting` for describing prior work, not StableSteering itself

## Steering notation

- first mention: `steering state z`
- later prose: `steering state`
- avoid switching to `control vector`, `control abstraction`, or `latent` unless explicitly needed

## Evidence framing

- use `qualitative case study` or `qualitative demonstration artifact`
- avoid `proof of superiority`, `best result`, or `validated method`

## Modularity framing

- use `implements interchangeable modules under one lifecycle`
- avoid language that implies comparative ranking unless backed by experiments
